Leadership Welcome

Welcome to Clarendon College's online education division. As the landscape of higher education transforms, our mission remains centered on expanding horizons, breaking geographic boundaries, and delivering robust instructional frameworks that match campus learning with virtual flexibility.

"Distance education shouldn't mean learning in isolation. It is an opportunity to cultivate a dynamic, accessible, and deeply interactive global intellectual community."

Our commitment is rooted in absolute transparency, technological agility, and educational standards that hold fast to academic integrity.

Core Online Pillars

Academic Rigor

Providing course models evaluated by state-standard benchmarks to ensure equivalence to on-site classes.
