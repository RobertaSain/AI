What is a "Source" anyway?

A source is where you get your knowledge. It could be a YouTube video, a TikTok, a newspaper story, a peer-reviewed study, or a blog post. In high school, you may have just searched Google and grabbed the first link. In college, that will result in bad grades. You must critically evaluate every source before letting it into your papers.
